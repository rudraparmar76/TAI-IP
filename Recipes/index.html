Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoya (reduced milk): It serves as the base ingredient and provides the rich, creamy texture to the Gulab Jamun.
  • Semolina (sooji): Semolina is used to bind the ingredients together and gives a slightly grainy texture to the Gulab Jamun.
  • Sugar syrup: A sugar syrup infused with cardamom or rose water is used to soak the fried Gulab Jamun, adding sweetness and flavor to the dessert.

Recipe

  1. Sugar syrup: A sugar syrup infused with cardamom or rose water is used to soak the fried Gulab Jamun, adding sweetness and flavor to the dessert.
  2. Shape it into small balls and fry until golden.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup and soak the fried balls.
  4. Serve delicious Gulab Jamun!

Barfii

Ingredients

  • Khoya (reduced milk): It forms the primary ingredient and gives the barfi its rich, creamy texture.
  • Sugar: Used to sweeten the barfi and create the desired level of sweetness.
  • Flavorings and toppings: Various flavorings like cardamom, saffron, or rose water, and toppings like chopped nuts are added to enhance the taste and presentation of the barfi.

Recipe

  1. Heat khoya in a pan until smooth.
  2. Add sugar and flavorings (cardamom, saffron, etc.), mix well.
  3. Pour the mixture into a greased tray, spread evenly.
  4. Garnish with chopped nuts, let it cool, and then cut into pieces. Enjoy your barfi
